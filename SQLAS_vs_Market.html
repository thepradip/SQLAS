Honest Gaps vs Competition

Community & Adoption
RAGAS has widespread adoption and published research. SQLAS is newer — technically strong but lower community trust and fewer real-world case studies.
LLM Judge Calibration
RAGAS published calibration against human annotations. SQLAS LLM-as-judge metrics have no published calibration — confidence intervals are unknown.
No Public Leaderboard
Spider/BIRD leaderboards let you compare against GPT-4o and SQLCoder baselines. SQLAS has no public leaderboard — benchmark scores can't be externally compared yet.
Integration Tests
140 unit tests, but no end-to-end integration tests against real PostgreSQL / MySQL / Snowflake. Production reliability across DB types is not formally verified.

Verdict

For production SQL agent evaluation — safety, guardrails, agentic quality, multi-dimensional correctness — SQLAS is the most comprehensive framework available. The safety and agentic metrics exist nowhere else. For academic NL2SQL benchmarking with published leaderboards, RAGAS and defog/sqleval remain more established.
